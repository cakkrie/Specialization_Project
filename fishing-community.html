Fishing Population are growing

Fishing participation is rising worldwide as more people turn to outdoor recreation for relaxation, community, and connection with nature. Millions of new anglers join each year, especially in regions where access to fishing is expanding.

(02)

Anglers Are Getting Younger

A new generation of passionate young anglers is reshaping the culture of fishing. Unlike traditional hobbyists, these digital-native anglers seek efficient, social, and data-driven experiences.

(03)

The Fishing Market Is Rapidly Expanding

Valued at around USD 567 billion in 2024, the global fishing market is projected to reach nearly USD 770 billion by 2029. Recreational and sports fishing gear continues steady growth, fueled by outdoor leisure and online retail trends.

(04)

Digitalization is reshaping the fishing experience

Smart devices, AI assistants, and mobile platforms are transforming how anglers connect, learn, and fish. This wave of innovation makes fishing more accessible, personalized, and community-driven—blending technology with tradition for a new era of angling.
